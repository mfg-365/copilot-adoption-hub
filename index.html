The journey

Four phases from readiness to scale

Each engagement aligns customer priorities to key success outcomes — ensuring effective design, adoption, and customers’ AI business solutions.

01Weeks 1–2

Assess & prepare

Survey employees, assemble the core team, review analytics, and run Data Readiness & Copilot Assurance workshops. Build the business case and confirm licensing.

02Weeks 3–5

Onboard & enable

Stand up the champion community, run a Prompt-a-thon, deliver foundational training, and host an Executive Immersion to secure sponsorship.

03Weeks 6–11

Deliver value & scale

Advanced training, line-of-business sessions (HR, Sales, Finance, Legal, Ops, Marketing), and Deliver/Govern/Build Agents workshops.

04Weeks 12+

Measure & extend

Run adoption & impact reports, deliver the IT/Executive readout, build agents, and keep momentum with continuous, on-demand training.

Meet users where they are

Five adoption personas

The schedule is organized into swimlanes so every audience gets the right depth of enablement — from broad awareness to specialist governance.

Awareness

Everyone

Broad comms, hints & tips, executive immersion and readouts that build understanding and excitement.

User

Licensed users

Foundational training and line-of-business sessions to build daily prompting habits.

Power User

Advanced adopters

Prompt-a-thons and advanced training on Notebooks, Create, and first-party agents.

Builder

Makers

Deliver/Govern/Build Agents workshops and hands-on agent builds.

Specialist

IT & governance

Data readiness, assurance, and agent governance to keep the rollout secure and compliant.

Champions & project

The connective tissue: the core team, champion community, comms cadence and stakeholder check-ins.
